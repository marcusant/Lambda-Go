λ

Lambda is an image and video hosting service. We try to be simple and unobtrusive, leaving the images as the centerpiece instead of ads and other nonsense. We also give really short links.

Lambda can be accessed by (and linked through) the following domains:
λ.pw
lambda.sx

Currently, Lambda supports png, gif, jpg, mp3, ogg, opus, mp4, and webm filetypes.
The max filesize allowed is 20MiB.

Lambda's paste service is always encrypted. All pastes are encrypted on the client end before being submitted for storage.
The paste service makes use of the Stanford JavaScript Crypto Library for client-sided crypto.

Keys are not stored anywhere. Keeping the links to your encrypted content is the only way to ensure you (or whoever it is shared with) can retrieve it.
